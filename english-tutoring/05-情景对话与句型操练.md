### 第五部分：情景对话与句型操练（12组日常场景｜替换练习｜口语打卡）
**使用方法**：每组对话先跟读 3 遍 → 分角色和老师/家长对练 2 遍 → 用「替换词」换词再练 2 遍 → 最后脱稿演一遍。每周完成 2-3 组。
---
#### 对话1：打招呼与自我介绍（Greetings）
> A: Hello! My name is Li Ming. What's your name?
> B: Hi! I'm Wang Fang. Nice to meet you.
> A: Nice to meet you, too. How old are you?
> B: I'm twelve. What about you?
> A: I'm thirteen. Which class are you in?
> B: I'm in Class Two, Grade Seven.
**中文**：你好！我叫李明，你叫什么名字？/ 嗨！我叫王芳，很高兴认识你。/ 我也很高兴认识你。你多大了？/ 我十二岁，你呢？/ 我十三岁。你在哪个班？/ 我在七年级二班。
**核心句型**：What's your name? / How old are you? / Which class are you in?
**替换操练**：把名字、年龄、班级换成自己的真实信息练习。
#### 对话2：介绍家人（Family）
> A: Is this your family photo?
> B: Yes, it is. This is my father. He is a doctor.
> A: Who is that woman?
> B: She is my mother. She works in a school.
> A: Do you have any brothers or sisters?
> B: Yes, I have a little sister. She is only five.
**核心句型**：Is this...? / Who is that...? / Do you have any...?
**替换操练**：doctor→teacher/worker/driver；little sister→big brother。
#### 对话3：课堂用语（In Class）——上课听懂老师必备
> T: Class begins. Good morning, class.
> S: Good morning, Miss Li.
> T: Please open your books and turn to page 25. Read after me.
> S: Sorry, Miss Li. I can't hear you. Could you say it again, please?
> T: Sure. Page twenty-five.
> S: Thank you. May I ask a question?
> T: Of course, go ahead.
**必背课堂指令**（听到要马上反应）：
Open your books. 打开书 / Close your books. 合上书 / Read after me. 跟我读 / Listen carefully. 认真听 / Hands up. 举手 / Be quiet. 安静 / Come to the blackboard. 到黑板前来 / Any volunteers? 有自愿的吗？
**学生求助句**：I have a question. 我有问题 / How do you spell it? 怎么拼写？/ What does it mean? 这是什么意思？/ Pardon? 请再说一遍？
#### 对话4：谈论学校生活（School Life）
> A: What's your favorite subject?
> B: My favorite subject is English. It's interesting. What about you?
> A: I like math best, because it's useful.
> B: Who is your English teacher?
> A: Miss Chen. She is very kind, but she is strict with us.
> B: When do you have English?
> A: We have it on Monday, Wednesday and Friday.
**核心句型**：What's your favorite...? / I like ... best, because... / be strict with 对……严格
**替换操练**：English→music/science/history；interesting→fun/easy/exciting。
#### 对话5：谈论时间与日程（Time & Daily Routine）
> A: What time do you get up on school days?
> B: I get up at six thirty. Then I have breakfast at seven.
> A: When do classes begin?
> B: At eight o'clock. We have seven classes every day.
> A: What do you do after school?
> B: I usually play basketball. Sometimes I go to the library.
**核心句型**：What time / When do you...? / I usually... / Sometimes I...
**频率副词阶梯**：always 总是 > usually 通常 > often 经常 > sometimes 有时 > never 从不（位置：be 动词后、实义动词前）。
#### 对话6：购物（Shopping）
> A: Can I help you?
> B: Yes, please. I want a schoolbag for my sister.
> A: What color does she like?
> B: She likes blue. How much is this blue one?
> A: It's sixty yuan.
> B: That's a little expensive. Do you have a cheaper one?
> A: What about this one? It's only forty yuan.
> B: Great. I'll take it. Here is the money.
**核心句型**：Can I help you? / How much is...? / I'll take it.
**替换操练**：schoolbag→T-shirt/hat/pencil box；blue→red/black；数字价格随意替换（练数字听说）。
#### 对话7：点餐（Ordering Food）
> A: Good evening. May I take your order?
> B: Yes. I'd like a hamburger and a glass of orange juice.
> A: Anything else?
> B: What's your soup of the day?
> A: Tomato soup. It's very popular.
> B: OK, a tomato soup, please.
> A: Sure. Please wait a moment.
**核心句型**：I'd like... (= I would like，比 I want 更礼貌) / Anything else? / Wait a moment.
**注意**：a glass of water 一杯水、a cup of tea 一杯茶、a bowl of rice 一碗饭——不可数名词用量词表达。
#### 对话8：问路（Asking the Way）
> A: Excuse me, how can I get to the bookstore?
> B: Go along this street and turn left at the second crossing. It's next to the bank.
> A: Is it far from here?
> B: No, it's about ten minutes' walk.
> A: Thank you very much.
> B: You're welcome.
**核心句型**：How can I get to...? / Go along... / turn left/right / next to / ten minutes' walk
**替换操练**：bookstore→hospital/post office/supermarket；left→right；second→first/third。
#### 对话9：谈论天气（Weather）
> A: What's the weather like today?
> B: It's sunny and warm. It's a good day for a picnic.
> A: What was the weather like yesterday?
> B: It was rainy and cold. I stayed at home all day.
> A: What will the weather be like tomorrow?
> B: The radio says it will be cloudy and windy.
**天气词**：sunny 晴 / rainy 雨 / cloudy 多云 / windy 有风 / snowy 下雪 / hot 热 / warm 暖 / cool 凉 / cold 冷
**语法亮点**：同一个问句的三种时态版本——is / was / will be，天然的时态对比练习。
#### 对话10：打电话（Making a Phone Call）
> A: Hello! May I speak to Tom?
> B: Hold on, please... Sorry, he isn't in. He is playing football outside. Who's that?
> A: This is Jack, his classmate. Could you ask him to call me back?
> B: Sure. What's your phone number?
> A: It's 138-2266-5588. Thank you.
**核心句型**：May I speak to...? / Hold on, please. / This is ...（电话中"我是"不用 I am）/ Who's that?（电话中"你是谁"不用 Who are you）/ call back 回电话
#### 对话11：谈论周末计划（Weekend Plans）
> A: What are you going to do this weekend?
> B: I'm going to visit the science museum with my cousin. Would you like to come with us?
> A: I'd love to, but I have to look after my little brother.
> B: What a pity! Maybe next time.
> A: Sure. Have a good time!
**核心句型**：be going to / Would you like to...? / I'd love to, but... （委婉拒绝三件套）/ What a pity! / Have a good time!
#### 对话12：看病（Seeing a Doctor）
> A: What's the matter with you, young man?
> B: I have a headache and a cough.
> A: How long have you been like this?
> B: Since yesterday morning.
> A: Let me see... It's nothing serious. You've just got a cold. Take this medicine three times a day, drink more water and have a good rest.
> B: Thank you, doctor.
**生病表达**：have a cold 感冒 / have a headache 头疼 / have a fever 发烧 / have a stomachache 胃疼 / have a cough 咳嗽
**医嘱表达**：take medicine 吃药 / three times a day 一天三次 / have a rest 休息

#### 附：万能句型替换操练表（每天 5 分钟）
以下每个句型，让学生口头替换划线部分，连说 5 个不同版本：
1. I like ~~English~~ very much. （music / playing basketball / my school / dogs / summer）
2. There is ~~a book~~ on the desk. （a pen / an apple / some water / a photo）
3. He is ~~taller~~ than me. （older / stronger / more careful）
4. I am going to ~~visit my grandparents~~ this weekend. （see a movie / do my homework / play football）
5. Could you please ~~open the door~~? （say it again / help me / pass me the salt）
6. If it ~~is sunny~~ tomorrow, I will ~~go to the park~~. （rains → stay at home / snows → make a snowman）
