**使用方法**：每学完一章立即完成对应练习。做错的题必须在「错题本」上抄题+写错因+一周后重做。正确率 ≥ 80% 才进入下一章。

## 练习一：音标（对应第一部分）

### A. 长短元音辨析：判断划线部分是长音还是短音
1. sh**ee**p（ ）  2. sh**i**p（ ）  3. f**oo**d（ ）  4. b**oo**k（ ）  5. c**ar**（ ）

### B. 选出划线部分发音不同的一项
6. A. cat  B. bag  C. name  D. map
7. A. see  B. she  C. big  D. bee
8. A. three  B. this  C. think  D. thank
9. A. cake  B. day  C. name  D. map
10. A. go  B. home  C. hot  D. boat

### C. 根据音标写单词
11. /siː/ ______  12. /bʊk/ ______  13. /kæt/ ______  14. /neɪm/ ______  15. /haʊs/ ______

**答案与详解**：
1. 长（/iː/ 绵羊）2. 短（/ɪ/ 轮船）3. 长（/uː/）4. 短（/ʊ/）5. 长（/ɑː/）
6. **C**（name 是 /eɪ/，其余是 /æ/）7. **C**（big 是 /ɪ/，其余是 /iː/）
8. **B**（this 是浊音 /ð/，其余是清音 /θ/——咬舌音必考辨析）
9. **D**（map 是 /æ/，其余是 /eɪ/）10. **C**（hot 是短音 /ɒ/，其余是双元音 /əʊ/）
11. see 12. book 13. cat 14. name 15. house
**易错提醒**：sheep/ship、food/book 这类长短音对比是听力和辨音题最爱考的，长音务必拉满。

## 练习二：名词与冠词（对应语法第一、二章）

### A. 写出复数形式
1. box → ____ 2. city → ____ 3. boy → ____ 4. knife → ____ 5. child → ____ 6. sheep → ____ 7. watch → ____ 8. foot → ____

### B. 用 a / an / the / 不填（/）完成句子
9. I have ______ apple and ______ banana.
10. ______ apple is red and ______ banana is yellow. （接上句）
11. He plays ______ basketball after ______ dinner.
12. She is ______ honest girl.
13. I get up at seven in ______ morning.
14. We learn ______ English at school.

### C. 改错（每句一处错误）
15. I drink two milks every day. → ______
16. There are many sheeps on the farm. → ______
17. This is an useful book. → ______

**答案与详解**：
1. boxes（x 结尾加 es）2. cities（辅音+y 变 ies）3. boys（元音+y 直接加 s）4. knives（f/fe 变 v 加 es）5. children（不规则）6. sheep（单复数同形）7. watches（ch 加 es）8. feet（不规则）
9. an, a（apple 元音音素开头）10. The, the（第二次提到，特指）11. /, /（球类、三餐前零冠词）12. an（honest 的 h 不发音，元音开头——必考特例）13. the（in the morning 固定搭配）14. /（语言前零冠词）
15. two milks → two glasses of milk（milk 不可数，用量词）16. sheeps → sheep 17. an useful → a useful（useful 开头发 /j/ 辅音音素——**看发音不看字母**的反向考法）

## 练习三：代词（对应语法第四章）

### A. 选择正确的代词
1. ______ (I / Me) am a student. Please help ______ (I / me).
2. This is ______ (she / her) book. The book is ______ (her / hers).
3. ______ (They / Them) are my friends. I often play with ______ (they / them).
4. Look at the dog. ______ (Its / It's) tail is short.
5. We enjoyed ______ (us / ourselves) at the party.

### B. 用 some / any 填空
6. I have ______ questions.
7. Do you have ______ brothers?
8. There isn't ______ water in the glass.

### C. 同义句转换
9. This is my pen. = This pen is ______.
10. That is her bag. = That bag is ______.

**答案与详解**：
1. I, me（句首主格，动词后宾格）2. her, hers（后有名词用形物代，无名词用名物代）3. They, them（介词 with 后用宾格）4. Its（"它的"，It's = It is）5. ourselves（enjoy oneself 固定搭配）
6. some（肯定句）7. any（疑问句）8. any（否定句）
9. mine 10. hers（名物代 = 形物代 + 名词）

## 练习四：一般现在时与三单（对应语法核心时态）

### A. 用所给动词的正确形式填空
1. He ______ (like) playing football.
2. My mother ______ (watch) TV every evening.
3. Lucy ______ (study) hard at school.
4. Tom ______ (go) to school by bike.
5. I ______ (do) my homework, and my sister ______ (do) hers, too.
6. The earth ______ (be) round.

### B. 变否定句和一般疑问句
7. She likes music. 否定：______ 疑问：______
8. They play games after school. 否定：______ 疑问：______

**答案与详解**：
1. likes 2. watches（ch 加 es）3. studies（辅音+y 变 ies）4. goes（o 加 es）5. do, does（主语不同，三单区别）6. is（客观真理用一般现在时）
7. She doesn't like music. / Does she like music?（借助 does 后，动词还原为原形 like——**最高频错误**：写成 Does she likes ×）
8. They don't play games after school. / Do they play games after school?

## 练习五：现在进行时与一般过去时（对应时态章节）

### A. 用现在进行时填空
1. Look! The children ______ (play) in the park.
2. Listen! She ______ (sing) an English song.
3. I ______ (do) my homework now.
4. He ______ (run) on the playground. （注意双写）

### B. 写出动词过去式
5. go → ____ 6. see → ____ 7. have → ____ 8. eat → ____ 9. stop → ____ 10. study → ____

### C. 用一般过去时填空
11. I ______ (visit) my grandparents last Sunday.
12. She ______ (buy) a new bag yesterday.
13. ______ you ______ (watch) the movie last night? — Yes, I ______.
14. He ______ (not go) to school yesterday because he was ill.

**答案与详解**：
1. are playing（Look! 是进行时标志）2. is singing（Listen! 标志）3. am doing 4. is running（run 双写 n）
5. went 6. saw 7. had 8. ate 9. stopped（双写 p）10. studied
11. visited 12. bought（buy 不规则）13. Did, watch, did（did 出现后动词还原原形）14. didn't go
**易错提醒**：一般过去时的否定和疑问一律用 did，动词必须变回原形——Did you went ×，Did you go ✓。

## 练习六：将来时、比较级、情态动词

### A. 一般将来时
1. I ______ (visit) the museum tomorrow.（用 will）
2. Look at the clouds! It ______ (rain) soon.（用 be going to）

### B. 比较级和最高级填空
3. My brother is ______ (tall) than me.
4. This question is ______ (easy) than that one.
5. Summer is ______ (hot) season of the year.
6. English is ______ (important) than music, I think.
7. Which is ______ (beautiful), the red one or the blue one?

### C. 情态动词选择
8. You ______ (must / can) finish your homework first, then you ______ (must / can) watch TV.
9. — Must I clean the room now? — No, you ______ (mustn't / needn't).
10. You ______ (mustn't / needn't) swim in the river. It's dangerous!

**答案与详解**：
1. will visit 2. is going to rain（有迹象的推测用 be going to）
3. taller 4. easier 5. the hottest（最高级带 the，hot 双写 t）6. more important 7. more beautiful（两者比较用比较级）
8. must, can 9. needn't（needn't 不必；mustn't 禁止——**中考必考辨析**）10. mustn't（表禁止）

## 练习七：完成时、被动语态、从句（初二初三综合）

### A. 现在完成时
1. I ______ already ______ (finish) my homework.
2. ______ you ever ______ (be) to Beijing?
3. He ______ (live) here since 2020.

### B. 被动语态
4. English ______ (speak) in many countries.
5. The classroom ______ (clean) by the students yesterday.
6. A new school ______ (build) next year.

### C. 从句综合
7. I don't know ______ (that / if / what) he will come tomorrow.（是否）
8. The boy ______ (who / which) is wearing a red T-shirt is my brother.
9. If it ______ (rain / rains / will rain) tomorrow, we will stay at home.
10. Could you tell me where ______ (is the library / the library is)?

**答案与详解**：
1. have, finished 2. Have, been（have been to 去过）3. has lived（since + 时间点是完成时标志）
4. is spoken 5. was cleaned（yesterday → 过去时被动）6. will be built（将来时被动）
7. if（表"是否"）8. who（先行词是人）9. rains（**主将从现**：if 从句用一般现在时）10. the library is（宾语从句必须**陈述语序**——最高频错误点）
